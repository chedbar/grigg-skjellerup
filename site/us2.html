Grigg Skjellerup Orbital Animation

I simulated the orbit of Grigg Skjellerup in a program called Universe Sandbox

The orbit is not exactly correct because I was unable to make it so that the comet was in the same place at the same time as it is in the real world, wich affects its orbit(26p has fallen into Jupiter's sphere of influence and that significantly changed its orbit. Chances are high that it will do something like that again in the future. My simulation will not be accurate after such an event and is not an accurate representation of where 26p is currently)
