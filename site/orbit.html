Grigg Skjellerup's orbit

Some facts about the comet's orbit:

  • Comet 26p's most recent orbital epoch was on October 1st, 2018
  • The farthest Grigg Skjellerup is from the sun is 4.9 AU
  • The closet it is the sun is 1.1 AU
  • The eccentricity (measure of how elliptical) of its orbit is 0.6631
  • The orbital period is 5.31 years
  • Orbital inclination is 22.36 degrees to the plan of the solar system
