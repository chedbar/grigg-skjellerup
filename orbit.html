Grigg Skjellerup's orbit

Comet 26p's most recent orbital epoch was on October 1st, 2018. The farthest Grigg Skjellerup is from the sun is 4.9 AU at its aphelion, the closet it is the sun is 1.1 AU at its perihelion. the semi-major axis of 26p is 3 AU. The eccentricity of its orbit is 0.6631. The orbital period is 5.31 years, and the orbital inclination is 22.36 degrees
